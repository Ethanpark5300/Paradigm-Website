About Us

We are a psychiatric outpatient medication management facility located in Chandler, AZ. We treat children, adolescents, and adults (age 6s to 64). We diagnose and treat a variety of disorders: Depression, bipolar disorder, and other mood disorders, anxiety, panic disorder, OCD, and PTSD, ADHD, oppositional defiant disorder, conduct disorder and other disruptive behaviors, autism spectrum and other development disorders. A psychiatric evaluation is done first by diagnostic interview, then regular follow up appointments to ensure the prescribed medications are working effectively. A referral to therapy can be provided if needed. To serve you better, we are offering Telemedicine Services, and you can see your psychiatrist from the comfort of your own home or at our office. Your mental health is important, and we are here to help you every step of the way.
