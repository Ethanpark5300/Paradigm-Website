About Us

We are a psychiatric outpatient medication management facility located in Chandler, AZ. We treat children, adolescents, and adults. A psychiatric evaluation is done first by diagnostic interview, then regular follow up appointments to ensure the prescribed medications are working effectively. A referral to therapy can be provided if needed. To serve you better, we are offering Telemedicine Services, and you can see your psychiatrist from the comfort of your own home or at our office. Your mental health is important, and we are here to help you every step of the way.

Conditions Treated and Evaluated

  • Depression
  • General Anxiety and Social Anxiety
  • Panic Disorder
  • Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Bipolar Disorder
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Personality Disorders
  • Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD)
  • Mood Disorders
  • Autism & Development Disorders
  • Schizophrenia
  • Psychosis
  • Phobias, Addictions, and Eating Disorders
