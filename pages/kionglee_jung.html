Kionglee Jung

DNP, PMHNP, FNP - BC

Kionglee (Lee) Jung is a Board Certified Psychiatric Mental Health and Family Nurse Practitioner. She graduated with her Doctor of Nursing Practice in Family Practice from Arizona State University in 2014 and completed her Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner Certificate. Her passion strongly lies in building strong patient-provider relationships while providing professional and empathetic care to patients and their families.


Through the years, she has gained her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the University of Massachusetts - Amherst and a diploma in Clinical Research from Toronto, Canada. Most of her nursing career of more than 10 years were in hospitals and clinical research trials located in Massachusetts and Toronto. She has settled down with her family in beautiful Arizona over 10 years ago. She speaks English, Korean and Malay.


Her focus is on treating a variety of psychiatric disorders that range in severity, including depression, anxiety, PTSD, ADHD, mood disorders, bipolar, and psychosis. Her many years of training, education, and experience allow her to effectively evaluate, diagnosis and treat her patients. She utilizes a combination of supportive therapy and medication management to achieve mental health stability and wellness in her patients. She creates a nonjudgmental environment and takes time with everyone to ensure there is a strong trust and connection.
