Telemedicine

Paradigm Psychiatry offers Telemedicine appointments for both Intakes and Follow Up visits. We use HIPAA compliant software to connect virtually online to interact and consult with our patients privately and securely. Patients are able to conveniently meet with their provider in the comfort of their own environment. You can use any device that has both audio microphone and video camera capabilities, such as your mobile phone device, iPad, laptop, or desktop computer. Telemedicine offers flexibility and convenience, it saves time, offers a solution for patients with transportation issues or mobility limitations, and reduces the risk of missed or cancelled appointments. These are just a few of the reasons why choosing to see a mental health professional online can be a good idea.

About

Paradigm Psychiatry is a telemedicine psychiatry clinic located in Chandler, AZ. We also offerin-office service, and day appointment may be available.
