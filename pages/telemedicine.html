Telemedicine

Paradigm Psychiatry offers Telemedicine appointments for both Intakes and Follow Up visits. We use HIPAA compliant software to connect virtually online to interact and consult with our patients privately and securely. Patients are able to conveniently meet with their provider in the comfort of their own environment.
